# Task-B media guide — skipper, instruction and group holidays

The site is designed to work before media is uploaded. sailing gradients remain visible when a file is missing.

## Homepage video

Upload both versions if possible:

```text
assets/video/task-b-homepage-hero.webm
assets/video/task-b-homepage-hero.mp4
```

Recommended source footage:

- Yacht visibly sailing or manoeuvring
- Sea, yacht and crew clearly visible
- 1–4 people may be present, but not posing at the camera
- Stable shot, drone tracking shot, cockpit-to-bow shot, or low side-angle
- No logos from another charter company prominently visible
- No drinking or party scene in the hero
- Leave visual breathing room in the left half for the headline

Technical target:

```text
Aspect ratio: 16:9
Resolution: 1920 × 1080 px
Duration: 8–18 seconds
Frame rate: 24, 25 or 30 fps
Audio: remove audio completely
Loop: beginning and ending should connect smoothly
WebM target size: 2–5 MB
MP4 target size: 3–7 MB
```

The MP4 should use H.264. The WebM should use VP9 if available.

The poster/fallback image is:

```text
assets/images/hero/task-b-hero-sailing.webp
```

Recommended size: 2000 × 1125 px, under 500 KB.

## Selection rules

- Use real Task-B work whenever possible
- Avoid using the same photo twice
- Avoid screenshots from WhatsApp or social media
- Avoid portrait phone images for landscape website slots
- Avoid strong filters, crushed shadows, and extreme saturation
- Confirm consent from recognisable people
- Remove metadata and location details when appropriate
- Do not imply Task-B owns yachts that are chartered from partners
