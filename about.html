About Task-B

A sailing-services company built around practical responsibility.

Technical understanding, qualified instruction, yacht operations and hospitality brought together in one professional service.

Founder

Bart Nieborg

Bart is a Dutch sailing professional with experience as a skipper, RYA cruising instructor, assistant charter-base manager, mechanical engineer and hospitality professional.

Task-B grew from the observation that sailing assignments often sit between categories. A yacht owner may need technical awareness as much as command. A couple may need patient instruction rather than someone simply taking over. A group holiday needs both capable skippers and thoughtful organisation.

Task-B is designed to cover that complete operational picture while remaining direct, adaptable and personal.

Technical foundationMechanical engineering experience and structured problem-solving
Operational experienceCharter, sailing-school and base-support environments
Guest understandingYears of hospitality and work with international crews

The company model

Task-B can deliver directly or coordinate the right team.

The first assignments may be led personally by Bart. Larger holidays, deliveries or overlapping projects can require additional skippers, instructors or crew. Task-B keeps responsibility for selection, briefing, communication and the agreed service standard.

The objective is not to look larger than the company is. It is to organise each assignment at the correct professional scale.

Working principles

Professional where it matters. Human where it counts.

Before the voyage

  • Clear scope and responsibilities
  • Honest assessment of fit and limitations
  • Structured preparation
  • Direct communication

During the voyage

  • Safe, calm decision-making
  • Respect for owners, students and guests
  • Adaptability without losing standards
  • Practical documentation where useful
