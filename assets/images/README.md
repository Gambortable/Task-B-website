# Task-B image slots

Place your own photographs in the folders below. The HTML expects `.webp` files; temporary SVG placeholders are shown automatically until the real files exist.

## Hero
- `hero/hero-professional-sailing.webp` — 2400 × 1500 px recommended; wide image with usable dark or quiet area on the left.

## Services
- `services/service-skipper.webp` — skipper actively commanding or manoeuvring.
- `services/service-instruction.webp` — clear teaching interaction.
- `services/service-delivery.webp` — passage-making, yacht preparation or arrival.
- `services/service-holidays.webp` — authentic group enjoyment with a sailing context.

## About
- `about/bart-at-helm.webp` — horizontal working image.
- `about/about-task-b.webp` — wide operational image.
- `about/bart-profile.webp` — portrait orientation, ideally 4:5.

## Details
- `details/qualifications.webp` — chartwork, briefing, instruments or passage preparation.
- `details/contact.webp` — chart, route planning or a calm onboard planning moment.

## Export guidance
- Use WebP at approximately 75–85% quality.
- Keep large hero images below roughly 500 KB where practical.
- Keep service-card images below roughly 250 KB.
- Do not place important faces or objects at the extreme edges; responsive crops use `object-fit: cover`.
- Replace files without changing the filenames, so no HTML edits are needed.
