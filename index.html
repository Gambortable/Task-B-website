About Task-B

Professional sailing with a practical and personal approach.

Task-B combines professional sailing, instruction, charter operations, mechanical engineering and hospitality. That combination supports safe decisions on board, clear teaching and reliable organisation before the trip.

RYA Yachtmaster Offshore Professional seamanship and command
RYA Cruising Instructor Practical tuition and crew development
Mechanical engineering Technical judgement and structured problem-solving
Charter & hospitality Operations, guests and international groups

Task-B does not claim to own a fleet. For organised holidays, suitable yachts are selected through charter partners according to destination, group size, comfort and budget.
