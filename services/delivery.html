Yacht Delivery

Structured yacht delivery from preparation to handover.

A professional relocation service built around yacht readiness, passage planning, communication and responsible command.

Discuss this assignmentView all services

Service overview

A safe delivery begins before departure. Task-B establishes the scope, reviews available yacht information, plans the passage and defines how progress, defects and decisions will be communicated.

  • Suitable forOwners, brokers, charter companies
  • CrewSkipper only or arranged crew
  • ReportingProgress and handover options
  • Go/no-goSubject to yacht and weather review

Delivery options

The right crewing model depends on yacht size, condition, route, season and expected passage duration.

  • Delivery skipper only
  • Delivery skipper with crew
  • Owner-assisted delivery
  • Coached delivery with tuition
  • Short coastal relocation
  • Longer Mediterranean or offshore passage

Typical preparation and reporting

The exact package is agreed per assignment, but can include the following components.

  • Pre-departure inspection within agreed scope
  • Document and equipment review
  • Passage and weather planning
  • Crew organisation and watch structure
  • Progress updates during the voyage
  • Defect and incident log
  • Arrival handover and delivery report

Clear limitations protect everyone

A delivery proposal records known yacht condition, inspection limits, owner responsibilities, weather assumptions and decision authority. This prevents the informal gaps that often create risk or disagreement during a relocation.

Clear scope, sound preparation and professional communication before the yacht leaves the berth.

Share the yacht, location, dates and objective.
