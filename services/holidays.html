Sailing Holidays

A sailing holiday designed around the group—not a template.

From one yacht with a skipper to a coordinated multi-yacht holiday, Task-B brings the people, vessels, route and practical structure together.

Discuss this assignmentView all services

Service overview

The best sailing holiday is not simply the yacht with the lowest price or the longest route. It is the right combination of group expectations, suitable vessels, capable skippers, realistic distances and well-chosen stops.

  • Suitable forFriends, families, companies, organisations
  • ScaleOne yacht or coordinated flotilla
  • PlanningYachts, crew, route and logistics
  • StyleActive, relaxed or mixed

What Task-B can organise

The service can cover the complete structure or a defined part of it, depending on what the group already has in place.

  • Understanding the group and objectives
  • Selecting suitable yachts
  • Finding and coordinating skippers
  • Designing the route
  • Planning activities and stops
  • Organising the practical structure
  • Coordinating several yachts
  • Delivering the holiday with Task-B personnel

Built for groups

Friends, families, companies and organisations all require different rhythms and levels of structure. Task-B plans around sailing ability, social atmosphere, privacy, comfort, budget and the amount of responsibility guests want to carry.

One responsible organiser

The commercial value is clarity: one party keeps an overview of the sailing plan, people, suppliers, skippers and practical decisions rather than leaving the group to coordinate separate pieces themselves.

Clear scope, sound preparation and professional communication before the yacht leaves the berth.

Build the right scope

Share the yacht, location, dates and objective.

Task-B will use those details to define responsibilities, preparation, availability and the most suitable service structure.
