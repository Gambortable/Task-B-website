Service overview

A skipper assignment is never only about steering the yacht. It is about taking responsibility for safe operation, sound decisions, clear communication and the people aboard.

  • Suitable forOwners, charter clients, groups
  • AssignmentSingle day to extended voyage
  • YachtsSailing yachts by prior review
  • LocationInternational by agreement

When skipper hire fits

Task-B can step aboard for a complete holiday, a defined passage, temporary relief, local cruising support or replacement cover. The exact role is agreed before the assignment so command, owner involvement and crew expectations remain clear.

  • Holiday skipper
  • Temporary or replacement skipper
  • Support for a demanding passage
  • Local cruising and harbour knowledge
  • Relief skipper for part of a voyage
  • Professional support for a charter group

What the service can include

The scope can range from command-only support to preparation, crew briefing and practical coaching during the voyage.

  • Pre-departure planning
  • Yacht and document review
  • Safety and crew briefing
  • Route and weather assessment
  • Harbour, anchoring and passage decisions
  • Post-voyage handover

A professional but adaptable approach

Some owners want a skipper to take full operational control. Others want to remain actively involved. Charter groups may need calm leadership without a formal atmosphere. Task-B defines that balance early and adapts the onboard style to the yacht, crew and purpose of the voyage.

Clear scope, sound preparation and professional communication before the yacht leaves the berth.
